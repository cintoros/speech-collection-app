Recoding/Labeling

After you have registered yourself successfully, you can start the labeling/recoding process.

Labeling

Click the menu-icon in the top left corner to expand the sidenav. After you click on "Check" you can begin with checking already matched snippets

Recoding

Click the menu-icon in the top left corner to expand the sidenav. After you click on "Record" There you can begin with recoding excerpts
In case you are labeling for a group you can press the private button in case the excerpt contains sensitive data.
